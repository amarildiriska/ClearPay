Everything the books need

One dashboard, not five spreadsheets.

Invoicing, quotes, clients, and reporting — connected, so nothing has to be re-typed twice.

Invoicing

Invoices clients actually pay

Build a line-item invoice in under a minute, apply tax or a card fee with a checkbox, and send it as a clean PDF or by email.

Quotes

Estimates that become invoices

Send a quote, get it approved, and convert it straight into an invoice — no retyping line items or amounts.

Clients

One directory for every client

Every job site, contact, and company in a searchable directory, linked to every quote and invoice tied to them.

Chart of Accounts

Consistent categories on every invoice

Set up the categories you bill under once, then pick from the same list every time you build a quote or invoice - no retyping, no inconsistent naming.

Reports

Cash flow, at a glance

See total received, total outstanding, and what's overdue, broken down without exporting a single spreadsheet.

Payments

Get paid by card, directly

Connect your ClearPay account with Stripe so clients can pay an invoice on the spot — funds land straight in your account, no middleman to chase.

Start to finish

Send the invoice. Get the payment. Move on.

First

Create the invoice

Pick the client, add what was done and for how much, and decide whether tax or a card fee applies.

Then

Send it their way

Once it's saved, send it to their inbox straight from ClearPay. They see exactly what's owed and why — no line item left unexplained.

Finally

Watch it get marked paid

Payments come in through Stripe or get logged by hand, and your outstanding balance updates on its own.

Payments run through Stripe

Card details never touch our servers — Stripe handles processing and PCI compliance, so client payments stay secure end to end.
